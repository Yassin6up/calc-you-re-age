Parchisi Rules

Objective:

Be the first player to move all 4 tokens from your home area to the center finish area.

How to Play:

  • Roll a 6 to move a token out of your home area
  • Move tokens clockwise around the board
  • Land on an opponent's token to send it back to their home
  • Land on your own token to create a "safe" position
  • Tokens in safe positions cannot be captured
  • You need exact dice roll to enter the finish area
  • Roll a 6 to get an extra turn

Special Rules:

  • If you roll three 6s in a row, your turn is forfeited
  • You must move a token if possible
  • If no moves are possible, your turn is skipped
